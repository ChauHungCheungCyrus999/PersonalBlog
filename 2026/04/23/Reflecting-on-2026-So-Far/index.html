Reason Why I Write This Blog

Tbh, I’ve always wanted to write a review blog to check what I did and what I can improve. I was thinking of doing this during the final exam and the FYP proposal period (my CS FYP is about sports! ^^). However, I’ll probably be too lazy to write this blog out. zZZ

There are only 2 weeks left until my internship ends. Time really flies. It has already been 8 months and 2 weeks. My first IT job.

A Day of Mine

Tbh, my life right now is surrounded by 3 things: sleep, study, eat. I wake up around 7:45, shower, and go to the exhibition centre by MTR, then go to work. Since my supervisor is sick and the exam is coming, I just study in my cube 😂🤣

When 1:00pm comes, I have my company lunch. My stomach is big, so I always eat more than 1 box of meal. Sometimes it tastes good, sometimes it tastes mid, but I need more protein. After finishing my meal, I rush to Wan Chai EFX to train shoulders and abs. Sometimes I do incline bench press. Then I go back to the company and study until 6:00pm, and BYE BYE. I go down and have a meal at Fairwood, then go to the gym. I found out that if I eat dinner after finishing gym, I can’t sleep early because the food needs to be digested. I go to sleep around 10:00pm.

Sleep

I prioritize sleep a lot recently because it really affects a lot. The first important reason is because it can decrease my testosterone level.

And low testosterone will cause depression and fatigue, which make me unable to think clearly and fast, and upset all day. Depression will cause my body to enter a state of chronic stress and raise cortisol levels, which then inhibit protein synthesis.

I really want to grow more muscle. Therefore, I have to put a lot of weight on it. I used to sleep in a noisy place, but after I changed to another place to sleep, the sleeping quality has improved a lot ^^

This is an interesting topic to talk about ngl. I am in Year 3 right now. I am wondering what I will do in the future. Probably IT or Disciplined Service. IT is probably chiller than Disciplined Service. This sounds like I am wondering what my future wife will look like 😂😂😂😂. I AIN’T A BIG FAN OF FORTUNE TELLER.
